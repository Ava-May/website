Ava May - QA Engineer

Hi, I’m happy to see you here!

I created this simple webpage from scratch to showcase some of my HTML, CSS, and JavaScript skills.

While you’re here, I’d like to share some of my skills and my perspective on testing today, as well as where I believe it’s heading.

My background includes manual and automated testing, covering functional, UI, API, regression, and performance areas. I build and maintain test plans, create automation when needed, ensure data consistency, and support seamless delivery within cross-functional teams, with additional experience using BDD with Cucumber and Gherkin, as well as TDD frameworks.

My toolkit includes Java, JavaScript, TypeScript, SQL, along with Jira, Selenium, Playwright, Postman, Git, Jenkins, and Chrome DevTools. I also incorporate AI-assisted methods to make testing more efficient.

Through my experience with both Selenium and Playwright, I’ve discovered that Playwright offers greater efficiency, speed, and flexibility, and I believe it represents the future of test automation. I’ve also been creating AI-powered agents using tools like Tabnine and Perplexity to accelerate testing and improve accuracy. I believe combining modern automation frameworks with AI is the next step in making testing faster, smarter, and better aligned with the needs of modern software development.
